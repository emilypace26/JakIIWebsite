Once Jak gets his hands on the Morph Gun, his aggressive new attitude reaches unprecedented heights. The morph Gun can be easily transformed into four different weapons, once the required color-coded upgrades have been obtained and fitted. Initially, only the Scatter gun mode can be used. You can also fire weapons whilst on foot or driving a hover-vehicle but not whilst riding the Jet Board. The four mods Jak could acquire were the red Scatter Gun, yellow Blaster, blue Vulcan Fury, and dark Peace Maker.

The yellow mod focuses on power and range, but at the expense of ammo efficiency and demanding more precise aiming. Blue Eco





Similar to the Blaster, the Vulcan Fury utilizes a laser sight for longer-range precision. It only differs in terms of its rate-of-fire, which in the case of the Vulcan Fury once warmed up, becomes a continuous stream of blue bullets. It is at this point where the accuracy from the laser sight becomes effectively useless, making it sub-par for sniping enemies. The sling blaster, spyder gunner, flying spider, early prototype roboguards, the blast bot, silverbacks, franken apes, and ghoul spiders are all highly vulnerable targets to the fast damage dealing capabilities of the Vulcan Fury.

Dark Eco





The dark mod focuses on power and destruction, at the considerable expense of ammo. With a decent rate of fire and range, these weapons are best reserved for highly chaotic situations and particularly tough enemies. The Peace Maker is the most powerful of the four tier one mods and can thus be considered a last resort type weapon, it is however much more useful at the start of a fight as the charge will arc and spread out when it hits an enemy or object. This arc deals the same damage as if it would have hit an enemy directly, meaning that the Peace Maker will be most effective when used at the start of a fight when there are more enemies for the arc to spread. Pretty much any medium-sized enemy will be killed in a single hit from the Peace Maker.
